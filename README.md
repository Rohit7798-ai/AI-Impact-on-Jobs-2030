# AI Impact on Jobs by 2030 

## Project Overview
This project analyzes how Artificial Intelligence (AI) may impact different jobs by the year 2030.  
The analysis focuses on automation risk, salary trends, AI exposure, skills, and future-proof careers.

## Dataset
- Source: AI_Impact_on_Jobs_2030.csv
- Records: 3000 jobs
- Features include:
  - Job Title
  - Average Salary
  - Education Level
  - Years of Experience
  - AI Exposure Index
  - Automation Probability (2030)
  - Risk Category
  - Skill Indicators (Skill_1 to Skill_10)

## Key Questions Answered
- What percentage of jobs are at low, medium, and high automation risk?
- Does higher salary mean lower automation risk?
- Is experience strongly related to salary?
- Which jobs are most and least exposed to AI?
- Which skills reduce automation risk?
- What are the most future-proof jobs?

## Key Insights
- Around 50% of jobs fall under medium automation risk.
- High-paying jobs are not always safe from automation.
- Experience shows little correlation with salary.
- Skills matter more than education alone.
- Jobs like Doctor, Research Scientist, Teacher, Nurse, and AI Engineer are more future-proof.
